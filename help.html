Studera Help

Use the school archive with confidence.

This guide explains the core Studera workflow: creating an account, joining the right school, posting threads, replying with uploads, saving useful discussions, and managing settings.

1. Create And Verify

Choose your school from the suggestion list, enter the school join key if one is required, then use the emailed verification code. Your profile is created only after verification.

2. Browse Your School

The Feed only shows discussions from your own school. Use curriculum filters, class filters, and search to find questions by title, body, author, section, or attachment name.

3. Start A Thread

Use New Thread, choose a section, write a clear question, and attach PDFs, images, documents, or other files when useful. You can remove attached files before publishing.

4. Reply And Upload

Open a thread to reply. Drag files into the attachment box or click it to choose files. Thread uploads are also collected under the Uploads tab for quick access.

5. Save And Solve

Bookmark important threads to keep them in your saved archive. Thread authors can mark a response as the solution when a reply fully answers the question.

6. Write Math

Studera renders LaTeX in threads and replies. Use inline math like $x^2$ or display math like $$E = mc^2$$ when a question needs formulas.

7. Report Issues

Use Report on threads or replies that need moderation. The report form scrolls on small screens, and school admins review reports without leaving the school archive.

8. Adjust Theme

Open Settings, then Appearance, to choose Light, Dark, or System theme. The setting stays in your browser so study sessions feel consistent.

Need Access?

Ask your school admin first.

If your school requires a join key, your school admin controls that key. If you cannot receive verification emails, check spam, school quarantine, and whether the email address was typed correctly.
